What We Collect

We use Microsoft PlayFab to manage player data and game services. When you play the game, we collect:

  • Custom ID: A unique, anonymous identifier used to save your progress and manage your game data on PlayFab.

We do not collect or store:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • IP addresses (we do not store or process them ourselves)
  • Location data
  • Voice or audio recordings
  • Biometric or personally identifiable information

How We Use Your Data

The Custom ID is used to:

  • Save and sync your game progress
  • Track gameplay statistics
  • Enable features like leaderboards or matchmaking (if implemented)

All data is stored securely on Microsoft PlayFab servers, which follow Microsoft’s own privacy and security policies.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

  • Microsoft PlayFab – Game backend services, data storage, and analytics.
